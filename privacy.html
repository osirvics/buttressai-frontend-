Privacy Policy

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using ButtressAI, including:

Your data helps us provide and improve the ButtressAI service:

  • Personalize your experience and content recommendations

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance app performance

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest

4. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

ButtressAI may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and other functions. These services are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only process data as instructed.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact our privacy team at privacy@buttressai.com
